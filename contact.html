Quick Answers

BEFORE YOU WRITE

How long does delivery take? +
Standard delivery is 2–4 business days across India. Same-day delivery is available in 20+ cities for orders placed before 2 PM. Express shipping (next day) is also available at checkout.
Can I return or exchange a product? +
Yes! We offer a 30-day hassle-free return window on all non-customised items. Simply initiate a return through your account or contact our support team. Customised items can only be returned for manufacturing defects.
How do I track my order? +
Once your order ships, you'll receive an SMS and email with a tracking link. You can also log into your KICKOFF account and check Order History for real-time updates.
Do you offer bulk / club discounts? +
Absolutely! We offer tiered discounts for orders of 10+ units. Fill in the Club & B2B enquiry form above or email clubs@kickoff.in and our team will get back to you with a custom quote within 4 hours.
How long does kit customisation take? +
Standard name and number customisation takes 48 hours. Complex designs, badge placements, or bulk club orders can take 5–7 business days. Rush options are available — mention your deadline in the form.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), all major credit and debit cards, net banking, and EMI options through major banks. All payments are 100% secure and encrypted.
