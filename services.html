FAQ

GOT QUESTIONS?

How long does customisation take? +
Standard customisation (name + number) takes 48 hours. Complex designs or bulk club orders may take 5–7 business days. You'll receive a confirmation email with the exact timeline after placing your order.
Can I return a customised item? +
Customised items are non-refundable unless there is a manufacturing defect or error on our part. Please double-check your name, number, and colour selection before placing the order. Standard (non-customised) items have a 30-day return window.
Which cities have same-day delivery? +
Same-day delivery is currently available in Hyderabad, Bangalore, Mumbai, Delhi, Chennai, Pune, and Kolkata. We're expanding to 15 more cities by end of 2026. Orders must be placed by 2 PM local time.
How does the boot fitting session work? +
In-store sessions include a 3D foot scan and gait analysis — takes about 25 minutes. Virtual sessions (via video call) use your measurements and playing style to recommend the best options. Both are free for Pro and Elite members.
Can I pause my membership subscription? +
Yes! Pro and Elite members can pause their subscription for up to 3 months per year without losing their tier benefits. Manage this directly from your account dashboard under "Membership Settings".
Do you supply kits for school and college teams? +
Absolutely! We have special institutional pricing for schools, colleges, and grassroots academies. Minimum order is 10 units. Contact our team via the Club Kits service form and we'll get back within 24 hours with a custom quote.
